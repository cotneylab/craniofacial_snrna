Summary statistics of a GWAS meta-analysis of facial shape phenotypes involving data from four cohorts of European descent (Rotterdam Study from the Netherlands, TwinsUK Study from the UK, ALSPAC Study from the UK, and Pittsburg Study from the USA, N=10,115) including 7,029,494 autosomal single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs) and 78 facial distance phenotypes obtained from 13 facial landmarks. See and cite publication, Xiong et al. Novel genetic loci affecting facial shape variation in humans. eLife 2019;8:e49898. DOI: https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.49898

Data interpret:

This file contains 7029495 rows:
   - one for header;
   - others for SNPs;
and 239 columns:
   - CHR, Chromosome;
   - BP, position (aligned according to NCBI human genome build GRCh37.p13);
   - SNP, SNP rsID;
   - EA, effective allele (minor allele);
   - OA, other allele;
   - BETA, SE, and P for each of 78 facial distance phenotypes.
